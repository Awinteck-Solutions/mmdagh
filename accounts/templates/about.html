Welcome To Ghana Agri Market



GHANA AGRI MARKET (GHAM) is one of the innovative e-commerce site developed and managed by Fintech Ghana Ltd. The easily accessible page creates lots of opportunities for farmers, SME’s, agri-businesses, NGO’s, Government agencies, traders and other related parties to trade their agricultural products, equipment and machineries on a secured environment, where transactions are carefully monitored to protect the interest of all parties.

With our vast range of products categories, clients and prospective clients can have access to most agricultural products locally and products yet to be imported to the country. GHAM is an interactive agri-business e-commerce site designed to break regional barriers and also help bring suppliers and buyers to one common platform to easily market their products.
